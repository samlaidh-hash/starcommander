# Loadout Items - Functions and Descriptions

## Default Equipment (Always Included)

### Shuttles
- **Default:** 2 shuttles (all factions except Trigon), 1 shuttle (Trigon only)
- **Function:** Multi-purpose craft with 6 mission types:
  - **ATTACK:** Engages enemy ships with beam weapons
  - **DEFENSE:** Intercepts torpedoes and enemy craft near mothership
  - **WILD_WEASEL:** Attracts enemy torpedoes away from ship
  - **SUICIDE:** Rams enemy ships for heavy damage
  - **TRANSPORT:** Delivers cargo to targets
  - **SCAN:** Scans locations for intelligence
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per shuttle
- **Launch:** Keys 1-6 (select mission, then press again to launch)

### Fighters
- **Default:** 1 fighter (Trigon only)
- **Function:** Fast, maneuverable combat craft
  - **ATTACK:** Engages enemy ships
  - **DEFENSE:** Protects mothership
  - **Beam Jamming:** Adds 0.3s cooldown to enemy beam weapons per hit
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per fighter
- **Launch:** CTRL + 1-6

### Mines
- **Default:** 1 mine (all ships)
- **Function:** Deployable explosive device
  - Detonates when enemies approach
  - Area damage on explosion
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per mine
- **Use:** Hotkey 3

## Additional Consumable Items

### Hull Repair Kit
- **Function:** Instantly restores +50 HP to ship hull
- **Use:** Hotkey 5
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per kit
- **Type:** Instant effect

### Energy Cells
- **Function:** Temporarily increases weapon damage by +20% for 60 seconds
- **Use:** Hotkey 6
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per cell
- **Type:** Time-based buff (60s duration)

### Extra Torpedoes
- **Function:** Adds +10 torpedoes to torpedo launcher storage
- **Use:** Hotkey 1
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per pack
- **Type:** Instant effect (adds to inventory)

### Extra Decoys
- **Function:** Adds +3 decoys to ship inventory
- **Use:** Hotkey 2
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per pack
- **Type:** Instant effect (adds to inventory)
- **Decoy Function:** Attracts enemy torpedoes and missiles

### Extra Mines
- **Function:** Adds +3 mines to ship inventory
- **Use:** Hotkey 3
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per pack
- **Type:** Instant effect (adds to inventory)

### Shield Boost
- **Function:** Permanently increases all shield facings by +20%
- **Use:** Hotkey 4
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per boost
- **Type:** Permanent upgrade

### Extra Shuttles
- **Function:** Adds +1 shuttle to bay capacity
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per shuttle
- **Type:** Adds to bay inventory

### Extra Fighters
- **Function:** Adds +1 fighter to bay capacity
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per fighter
- **Type:** Adds to bay inventory

### Extra Bombers
- **Function:** Adds +1 bomber to bay capacity
- **Bay Cost:** 2 spaces per bomber (bombers are larger)
- **Type:** Adds to bay inventory
- **Bomber Function:** Heavy combat craft with torpedoes and beams

### Extra Drones
- **Function:** Adds +1 drone to bay capacity
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per drone
- **Type:** Adds to bay inventory
- **Drone Function:** Automated defense/attack craft

### Extra Probes
- **Function:** Adds +1 probe to bay capacity
- **Bay Cost:** 1 space per probe
- **Type:** Adds to bay inventory
- **Probe Function:** Reconnaissance and scanning

## Bay Space by Ship Class

- **Frigate (FG):** 2 spaces
- **Destroyer (DD):** 4 spaces
- **Light Cruiser (CL):** 6 spaces
- **Heavy Cruiser (CA):** 8 spaces
- **Strike Cruiser (CS):** 8 spaces
- **Battlecruiser (BC):** 10 spaces
- **Battleship (BB):** 12 spaces
- **Dreadnought (DN):** 14 spaces
- **Super Dreadnought (SD):** 16 spaces

## Default Loadout Calculation

**All Ships (except Trigon):**
- 2 Shuttles (2 spaces)
- 1 Mine (1 space)
- **Total Default:** 3 spaces used
- **Available for Selection:** Bay Size - 3

**Trigon Ships:**
- 1 Shuttle (1 space)
- 1 Fighter (1 space)
- 1 Mine (1 space)
- **Total Default:** 3 spaces used
- **Available for Selection:** Bay Size - 3
